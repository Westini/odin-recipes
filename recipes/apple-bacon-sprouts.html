Apple bacon sprouts

Description:

This delicious side dish, breakfast, or even a snack will blow you away with flavor. from the delicious taste of bacon with a little hint of sweetness from the apple to the amazing flavor of sauteed brussel sprouts all combined to make a stunning and simple dish.

Ingrediants:

  • 4 slices of thick cut bacon
  • 1/4 cup Apple of choice (I chose ambroisa)
  • 1 cup brussle sprouts
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp bacon fat
  • apple cider vinegar to taste
  • salt to taste
