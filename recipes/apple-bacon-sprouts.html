Apple bacon sprouts

Description:

This delicious side dish, breakfast, or even a snack will blow you away with flavor. from the delicious taste of bacon with a little hint of sweetness from the apple to the amazing flavor of sauteed brussel sprouts all combined to make a stunning and simple dish.

Ingrediants:

  • 4 slices of thick cut bacon
  • 1/4 cup Apple of choice (I chose ambroisa)
  • 1 cup brussle sprouts
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp bacon fat
  • apple cider vinegar to taste
  • salt to taste

How to cook

Start by dicing the bacon and searing in a pan

Once bacon is crispy, drain the fat into a seperate bowl

Using the bacon fat, saute the brussel sprouts

Once the brussel sprouts start to brown, add the diced apple

Add apple cider vinegar to desired taste as well as salt and pepper

Once the sprouts and apple are cooked, add the bacon back in

Mix in the bacon and cook to desired crispiness. Enjoy
