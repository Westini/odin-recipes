Easy Omelette

Description of the dish

A classic morning breakfast made with one simple ingrediant, eggs.

Ingrediants

  • 2 eggs
  • Cheese of choice
  • Butter or olive oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste
  • Parsley

How to cook

Add 1 tbsp of butter or add oil to your pan.

Add two eggs and whirl around the pan.

Add 1 tbsp of butter

Add a 3/4 oz of cheese and mix

Keep mixing until desired consistency and enjoy
