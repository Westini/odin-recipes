Short rib stew

Description of the dish

This dish is made from short ribs that were browned and then simmered in chicken broth or broth of choice then mixed with a few delicious vegetables

Ingrediants

  • 4 beef short ribs
  • Chicken stock
  • Carrots
  • Green onion
  • Bok choy
  • Spices to taste
  • Minced garlic
  • Ginger
  • Lime

Cooking Instructions:

Start with browning the short rib all around with desired spices.

add the minced garlic and diced ginger to the beef

add diced carrots and enough chicken broth to cover the beef.

Let simmer for 2 hours

Add chopped green onion and bok choy

simmer another 45 mins - 1 hour

ENJOY!
